Selected Publications

Efficient Transformer Architectures for Resource-Constrained Environments

Dan, F., Johnson, R., & Lee, J. (2025). NeurIPS 2025.

We introduce a novel attention mechanism that reduces the computational complexity of Transformer models from O(n²) to O(n log n) while maintaining comparable performance on benchmark tasks. Our approach enables deployment on edge devices with limited computational resources.

Paper Code

Self-Supervised Visual Representation Learning with Minimal Labels

Dan, F., Wang, L., & Smith, T. (2025). CVPR 2025.

This paper presents a novel approach to self-supervised learning that requires only a small fraction of labeled data to achieve state-of-the-art performance on image classification tasks. Our method combines contrastive learning with a new semi-supervised fine-tuning strategy.

Paper Code

Attention-Guided Image Segmentation for Medical Imaging

Chen, H., Dan, F., & Miller, K. (2025). CVPR 2025.

We propose a novel attention mechanism for medical image segmentation that significantly improves accuracy in identifying small lesions and abnormalities. Our approach outperforms existing methods on multiple medical imaging datasets, including MRI, CT, and ultrasound.

Paper Code

Cross-lingual Transfer Learning for Low-resource Languages

Dan, F., Garcia, M., & Patel, S. (2024). ACL 2024.

This work addresses the challenge of building effective NLP systems for languages with limited training data. We introduce a novel cross-lingual transfer learning approach that leverages knowledge from high-resource languages to improve performance on low-resource languages.

Paper Code

Ethical Considerations in Deploying AI Systems: A Framework for Practitioners

Dan, F., Roberts, A., & Kim, J. (2024). FAccT 2024.

We present a comprehensive framework for addressing ethical concerns in the development and deployment of AI systems. Our approach integrates considerations of fairness, accountability, transparency, and privacy throughout the AI lifecycle, from data collection to model deployment and monitoring.
